About GenomicsViz

GenomicsViz is a powerful tool designed to help researchers visualize and analyze cancer genomics data. Our platform enables easy exploration of gene expression patterns, DNA mutations, and differential expression analysis through intuitive interactive visualizations.

Our mission is to accelerate cancer research by providing accessible and powerful tools for genomic data analysis.
